## About This Project

This is the official web presence of Sacred Daniel's Empire, a sovereign maritime nation. The website serves as:
- A digital embassy for international relations
- An archive of national documents and history
- The primary contact point for citizenship inquiries
- A platform for imperial communications

## Key Sections

1. **About Us** - National overview and cultural identity
2. **History** - Chronicles of our empire's foundation
3. **Codex** - The fundamental legal document
4. **Government** - Structure of imperial authority
5. **Media** - Official news and announcements
6. **Contacts** - Diplomatic and citizenship channels

## Technical Notes

- Static HTML/CSS website (no backend)
- Fully responsive design (works on mobile/desktop)
- Hosted on [GitHub]
- All documents linked via Google
